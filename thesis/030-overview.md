## Overlay Networking

The Open Systems Interconnection (OSI) model distinguishes 7 layers in computer networks:

- 7 - Application layer
- 6 - Presentation layer
- 5 - Session layer
- 4 - Transport layer
- 3 - Network layer
- 2 - Data link layer - 
- 1 - Physical layer
